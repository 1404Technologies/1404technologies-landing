## OmniServe (the product)

OmniServe is a unified operations platform that eliminates software sprawl
and connects your business tools — from project management and CRM to
accounting and compliance — in one intelligent hub. Built for UK and U.S.
SMEs, it automates workflows, provides real-time cross-system dashboards,
and ensures audit-ready compliance.

### Key features

- Universal integration layer (Xero, QuickBooks, Salesforce, Trello, MS Project, and 50+ more)
- No-code workflow automation
- Unified dashboards combining operational and financial data
- SaaS management module to discover and optimize subscriptions
- Compliance engine with audit trails and MTD/HIPAA/GDPR support
- Industry modules for Construction, Logistics, and Healthcare

### Client benefits

- Save up to 5 hours per user per week
- Reduce SaaS waste by identifying unused licenses
- Achieve audit-ready compliance automatically
- Connect field teams to office systems in real time
